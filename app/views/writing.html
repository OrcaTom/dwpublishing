DWP content on GOV.UK

The Government Digital Service (GDS) is solely responsible for how digital content intended for citizens or small businesses (known as 'mainstream') is presented on the single government website.

The GDS content team designs pages and content based on user insight, data and analytics to best meet user needs. All content must meet the design principles and style guide.

The DWP digital publishing team publishes PDFs, press releases and other data or non-mainstream content to GOV.UK.

This content must still meet the proposition of what should and shouldn't go on GOV.UK.

The title, description and location of this content on GOV.UK is decided by the DWP digital publishing team according to GOV.UK design principles and style guide.
