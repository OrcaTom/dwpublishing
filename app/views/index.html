DWP content on GOV.UK

The Government Digital Service (GDS) manages GOV.UK and sets the standard for how all content is presented on the single government website.

GDS has control over who can publish on GOV.UK and has the right to remove, refuse or change any content on GOV.UK that doesn't meet the standard.

The GOV.UK content team at GDS is solely responsible for any content intended for citizens or small businesses ('mainstream').

Departments are responsible for ensuring content on mainstream GOV.UK is factually accurate - not for the words used on the page.

The Digital Publishing team at DWP are responsible for placing departmental publications and specialist content onto GOV.UK. They do this to GDS guidelines.

Above everything, any content on GOV.UK must have a genuine and proven user need for it.
