DWP content on GOV.UK

The Government Digital Service (GDS) manages GOV.UK and is solely responsible for how content is presented on the single government website.

Above everything all GOV.UK content must have a genuine and proven user need.

Departments are responsible for checking content on GOV.UK is factually accurate - not for the words used on the page.

Nobody in DWP has 'sign-off' or ownership of any content on GOV.UK.
