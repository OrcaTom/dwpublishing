DWP content on GOV.UK

The Government Digital Service (GDS) manages GOV.UK and sets content standards.

There are strict rules about what can go on GOV.UK. All content on GOV.UK must have a proven user need.

GDS can remove or change any content on GOV.UK if it doesn't meet the standards.

The digital publishing team manages the department's requests for changes to GOV.UK.

If you're writing or planning content for GOV.UK without talking to the digital publishing team you should stop this work and contact Keith Prust (keith.prust@dwp.gsi.gov.uk).
